About Me

Hello,
This is Berfin. It's my 3rd year in Canada. I'm studying IMD at Algonquin college.
I've always been on a journey of self-discovery, and along the way, I've found myself jumping from one hobby to another, constantly changing my mind, eager to try something new, but never quite feeling satisfied. That’s how I started learning learning web development and realized I actually really like it.

This is where I started looking for a program that would provide a space to explore my artistic abilities further and grow, while also developing the many other skills I haven’t fully mastered yet. A place where I could make the most of what I’ve accomplished so far, whether it’s illustrating, drawing, sewing, crafting, web design, or photography which I have never considered learning.

It's through the experience that true growth unfolds.
